3) Click on “Add Token” on the bottom on the same page.

4) Fill in Token Contract Address with: x And you will see the Token Symbol and Token Decimal have been automatically filled in once the Token Contract Address has been filled.

5) Click on “next” and “Add Tokens” .

6) CST token should appear in your wallet alongside your other tokens.
