There are THREE steps to get ready

1. Install MetaMask on your Google Chrome Browser
2. Add Columbia Sports Tokens on the Rinkeby Test Network
3. Email your account address to cupm2021@gmail.com


STEP ONE: Metamask Installation

Get to Chrome web store extensions section and search MetaMask, or you can click here:
Metamask Quick Link

Here is a short video how you may setup your Metamask

STEP TWO: Add Columbia Sports Token
Step1:
click on “Ethereum Mainnet” at the upper right-hand corner and select the “Rinkeby Test Network”



Step 2:
Click on “Add Token” on the bottom on the same page.
Fill in Token Contract Address with: 0xb41E0D7463F11D3e772C8B3aD3b9bea6DAb03D62
And you will see the Token Symbol and Token Decimal have been automatically filled in once the Token Contract Address has been filled.




Click on “next” and “Add Tokens” ->



STEP THREE: Email Your Account Addres
Step1:
Click on “Account 1” to copy you address

Step 2:
Send your account address to cupm2021@gmail.com so that we can grant your access to Columbia Prediction Markets!
